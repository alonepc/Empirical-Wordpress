== Description ==

BP Auto activate  Autologin Redirect To Profile On Signup is a WordPress Mu(wpmu) and Buddypress plugin, which makes the registration process on a buddypress site super cool!. It activates user account instantly, after they click on signup complete button, then automatically logs them in to the site and redirects them to their profile.
So all your user sees is click Signup button and he is at his profile page,that too with logged in state.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the bp-auto-activate-autlogin folder to the plugins directory in your WPMU installation
2. Activate the plugin Sitewide
3. Have a nice time, let your users enjoy the instant activation  and the better feeling of getting redirected to their profile on signup
